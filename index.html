About me

I am a third-year PhD student at University of Amsterdam (UvA) and also a member of ELLIS society, working on Meta-learning. I am grateful to be supervised by Cees Snoek. I also received guidance from Xiantong Zhen. Prior to that, I completed my master’s degree in Software Engineering at Beihang University, China. My research interests are: meta learning, few-shot learning, memory-augmented method, and variational inference.
